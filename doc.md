WHO WE ARE
Welcome to The Way to God Gospel Ministry (WAGGOM).
WAGGOM is an interdenominational Christian ministry, a family of believers from different church backgrounds united by a common passion for Christ and His kingdom.
Founded on September 14, 1981, WAGGOM is a missions-oriented ministry with a vision to reach the unreached with the Gospel of Jesus Christ and to help existing denominations grow into the full image and stature of our Lord Jesus Christ.
Our covenant with God is centered on holiness, and our motto is taken from John 12:32:
"And I, if I be lifted up from the earth, will draw all men unto me."

OUR BELIEFS
Our faith is firmly rooted in the teachings of the Holy Scriptures.
The Holy Scriptures
We believe that the Bible is the infallible Word of God, eternally reliable in all matters concerning the Christian faith and life.
(2 Timothy 3:16)
The Trinity
We believe in the Divine Trinity, God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit—three distinct persons perfectly united as one God.
(Matthew 3:16–17; Matthew 28:19)
Repentance
We believe in true repentance, which involves godly sorrow for sin and a sincere turning away from it.
(Romans 5:1; 1 Corinthians 6:11)
Justification by Grace
We believe in justification and the remission of sins through God's grace, enabling believers to stand guiltless before Him.
(Ephesians 2:5–8)
The Baptism of the Holy Spirit
We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit, with speaking in tongues as one of its evidences.
(Acts 1:8; Mark 16:17)
The Church
We believe that the Church is the body of Christ, and all believers are united in Him.
(Romans 12:5)
Water Baptism
We believe in water baptism by immersion after repentance and full surrender to Jesus Christ.
(Matthew 28:19; Acts 8:28–38)
Divine Healing
We believe that divine healing is available to God's children through the finished work of Christ on the cross.
(Isaiah 53:4–5; James 5:14–16)
Life After Death
We believe in the immortality and consciousness of the spirit after physical death.
(Ecclesiastes 12:7)
The Second Coming of Christ
We believe in the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, the rapture of the Church, and the bodily resurrection of both the just and the unjust.
(Acts 1:9–11; 1 Thessalonians 4:15–17)
Heaven and Hell
We believe that Heaven is the eternal dwelling place for God's children, while Hell is the eternal destiny for those who reject Him.
(Matthew 25:41–46; Luke 16:24)

WAGGOM MISSIONS
"You shall be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth."
— Acts 1:8
Every believer is called to be a witness of Jesus Christ, beginning from their immediate environment and extending to the farthest parts of the world.
At WAGGOM, missions is at the heart of what we do. We believe that having received the free gift of salvation, we must also share the good news of hope and redemption with others.
Our approach to missions is holistic, combining the preaching of the Gospel with compassionate outreach—bringing hope to the poor, healing to the brokenhearted, deliverance to the captives, sight to the blind, and freedom to the oppressed.
(Luke 4:18)
With active missionaries serving in mission fields across interior regions of Nigeria, we are committed to fulfilling Christ’s mandate of taking the Gospel to the ends of the earth.
(Acts 13:47)
In partnership with Waggom Theological Seminary (WATHS), we operate a School of Missions, where individuals are trained and equipped for the work of end-time missions. Students participate in annual outreach missions to the hinterlands, gaining practical experience and developing a passion for souls.
As promised in Scripture, we continue to witness signs, miracles, and powerful testimonies wherever the Gospel is preached.
(Mark 16:15–18)
